## Moon & Myth

Moon & Myth is a prototype science exhibit designed by Andrew Patra, Justin Ferland, Isabella Consentino, and Minh Nguyen for ATLS 5519: Design A Science Exhibit - a class taught my Mark Gross at the University of Colorado Boulder. 

Moon & Myth explores how scientific phenomena on the Moon was explained through history, comparing ancient mythology to scientific discovery. The project is built in TouchDesigner using computer vision powered by MediaPipe, projection mapping, and custom content developed by the team.
